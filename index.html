Youth leader of Morbi, Gujarat – Pratham Amrutiya

Introduction

Youth leader born in Morbi, Gujarat. He is the son of Popular BJP leader and 7 term MLA from Morbi – Maliya Constituency Shri Kantilal Shivlalbhai Amrutiya, he is also close aide of our Hon. PM Modi Ji.

From very young age he has been involved in connecting people from Ground level and have always supported idea’s of Youth and followed the principle of #SevaParmoDharma. He is also an inspiration for Youth who are engaged in Social activities. In the year 2019 Zero use of Plastic campaign was launched by him by supporting the initaitive of Gujarat Government #PlasticBan. At that time leading with an example 10,000 cloth bags were distributed with the idea of making GUJARAT free from Plastic use. He has been honored with several awards for services during Lockdown and pandemic.
